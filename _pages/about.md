I am a bioinformatician and data analyst with a [Doctoral Degree in Biomedicine](http://hdl.handle.net/10201/152160) by the [University of Murcia](https://www.um.es/), during which I have been serving for the Rare Diseases Spanish Research Network ([CIBERER](https://www.ciberer.es/)). My area of expertise includes Hematological diseases, liver studies, use of long reads sequencing systems, development of *in‑silico* pipelines for both short and long reads and analysis of (epi)genetic, (epi)transcriptomic and clinical data from different cohorts of patients. Furthermore, I have been part of a number of competitive projects as member of the research team, and worked as collaborator with several research groups.
